# Dragon Cards

A Slay the Spire-inspired dragon card game. Mobile-first, playable in the browser.

**Play it:** [GitHub Pages](https://arthurbikard.github.io/Dragon/)

## Game Modes
- **VS Dragon (Solo)** - Fight 3 increasingly difficult dragon enemies. Choose cards between battles.
- **Local 2-Player** - Take turns on the same device.

## How to Play
- Pick an element (Fire, Water, Earth, Air) to get a themed starter deck
- Each turn: spend energy to play cards, then end your turn
- Attack cards deal damage, Block cards prevent damage, Skill cards provide special effects
- Elements have strengths: Fire > Earth > Air > Water > Fire (1.5x damage)
